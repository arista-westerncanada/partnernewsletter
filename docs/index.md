## Co-Sell Motions

### Campus in a Box — Compugen

A modular, pre-validated campus switching bundle for municipalities, school boards, and mid-market enterprise. Sold through Compugen. Available via Canoe Procurement Group for public sector customers.

| Size | Bundle | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Small | 3-switch pack | Town halls, community centres |
| Medium | 5-switch pack | Schools, municipal offices |
| Large | 10+ switch pack | Multi-site deployments |

### Oil & Gas Ruggedized Bundle — Clutch Solutions

Purpose-built for Alberta upstream and midstream operators. Ruggedized Arista switching for harsh environments combined with EOS streaming telemetry for remote site visibility.

- Single EOS platform across HQ and remote field sites
- CloudVision zero-touch provisioning
- Available through Clutch Solutions for Alberta accounts
